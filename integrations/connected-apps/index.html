    Connected Apps¶

    Ravian connects to your core productivity and collaboration tools so it can read, write, and coordinate work across them as part of a single autonomous workflow.

    To manage integrations, open Settings → Connections inside the Ravian application.

    Available integrations¶


    • Google Drive
      Store, read, and organize reports, datasets, and workflow artifacts directly in Drive.


    • Notion
      Generate pages, update knowledge bases, and log structured research outputs.


    • Google Calendar
      Schedule meetings, coordinate follow-ups, and manage automation-driven events.


    • GitHub
      Create issues, draft pull requests, and maintain documentation and code.


    • Twitter / X
      Draft posts, analyze conversations, and summarize discussion threads.


    • Google Docs
      Draft, revise, and maintain long-form collaborative documents.


    • Salesforce
      Sync customer records, update opportunities, and automate CRM workflows.


    • Gmail
      Draft, organize, and automate email communication with full approval control.


    • YouTube
      Analyze videos, generate summaries, and prepare content workflows.


    • LinkedIn
      Draft posts, analyze profiles, and automate professional outreach workflows.


    • PPT
      Create, Redesign PowerPoint Presentations.

    What Ravian can do with integrations¶

    Once connected, Ravian can coordinate actions across multiple apps within a single workflow.

    A single task can, for example, collect research from the web, draft a report in Google Docs, store artifacts in Google Drive, log decisions in Notion, schedule follow-up meetings in Google Calendar, and open GitHub issues based on findings.

    All actions remain visible, traceable, and controllable throughout execution.

    Using integrations safely¶

    Ravian enforces strict control boundaries when working with connected services.

    Human approval is required for sending messages, modifying shared content, or performing sensitive actions.
    Access can be limited to specific accounts, folders, or repositories.
    All integration activity is recorded in the execution history.

    Only connect the services required for your workflows.

    Adding more integrations¶

    Ravian’s integration framework is designed to scale.

    New integrations can be added without changing your existing workflows. Each integration will appear in this section with supported actions and any required setup steps.
