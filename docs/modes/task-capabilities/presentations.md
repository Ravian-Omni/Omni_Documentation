# Presentation Generation

Ravian AI creates **end-to-end presentations** from research, data, or instructions, combining content creation with professional design.

Give Ravian a topic or dataset; get a complete presentation ready for stakeholders.

---

## What this capability does

Task Mode presentation workflows:

- Research topics and synthesize into structured slide narratives.
- Turn datasets into charts, tables, and executive summaries.
- Generate speaker notes, animations, and professional layouts.
- Export to PowerPoint, Google Slides, or PDF formats.
- Update decks automatically when source data changes.

!!! note
    Task Mode tracks all source materials and data lineage for auditability.

---

## Typical workflows

Presentation use cases:

- Market landscape and competitor analysis decks.
- Quarterly business reviews from operational data.
- Technical architecture overviews for engineering teams.
- Sales proposals and customer-facing pitch decks.

---

## Example: Quarterly business review

1. Launch the task:

    ```text title="Prompt"
    Create a Q4 business review ppt from sales.csv, support_metrics.xlsx, and customer_feedback.json.
    ```

2. Ravian analyzes all inputs, identifies key trends, and structures 15 slides covering revenue, customer health, and operations.

3. It generates charts, pulls relevant quotes, and formats professionally.

4. PPT is saved to your workspace with all source data attached.

From raw data to presentation in one workflow.
