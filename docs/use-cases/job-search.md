# Job Search Automation

Ravian AI turns your **job search** into an automated pipeline, from scanning roles to drafting and submitting applications where you approve.

You define your profile and preferences; Ravian takes care of repetitive browsing and form filling.

---

## What this capability does

Ravian's job search automation:

- Searches across job boards using your criteria (title, location, salary, companies).
- Filters and ranks opportunities automatically.
- Fills application forms using your resume and preferences.
- Drafts tailored cover letters or responses.
- Tracks applications and maintains a master log.

!!! warning
    Always review and approve applications before final submission.

## Getting Started

### Step 1 - Configure your job preferences

    
    Set my job preferences:
    - Role: Data Scientist or ML Engineer
    - Location: Bangalore or Remote (India)
    - Salary: 15+ LPA
    - Experience: 2–5 years
    - Resume: ~/Documents/resume.pdf
    

---

### Step 2 - Create the automation task

    
    Find 10 Data Scientist roles on Naukri.com matching my preferences
    and prepare applications for approval.
    

---

### Step 3 - Review the plan

Before execution, Ravian shows:

* Target job boards
* Search filters applied
* Form filling strategy
* Cover letter templates

Approve the plan once it looks correct.

---

### Step 4 - Execute with approvals

After approval, Ravian:

* Searches job boards
* Identifies matching roles
* Fills application forms
* Uploads your resume
* Drafts personalized cover letters
* Pauses for approval before each submission

You stay in full control.

---

### Step 5 - Results and tracking

After execution:

* Ravian generates a spreadsheet with:

  * Company
  * Role
  * Application status
  * Follow-up date

* Ravian highlights pending actions and next steps.

## Pro Tips

> Small tweaks that make your job automation dramatically more effective.

### Daily automation

    Run this workflow every weekday at 9 AM.


### Multiple job boards

    Include LinkedIn, Indeed, and company career pages.


### Cover letter templates

    Use template A for startups and template B for enterprise companies.


### Follow-up automation

    Two weeks after applying, send a polite follow-up email.


### Interview preparation


    After interviews are scheduled, research each company and prepare questions.
